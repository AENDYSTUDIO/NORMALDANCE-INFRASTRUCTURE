### Incident Detection (0-5 minutes)
- [ ] **Immediate Response**
  - [ ] Acknowledge incident
  - [ ] Assess severity level
  - [ ] Notify on-call team
  - [ ] Activate emergency procedures

### Incident Response (5-30 minutes)
- [ ] **Containment**
  - [ ] Isolate affected systems
